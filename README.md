# Staff KPI Performance Tracker — Main Location

## Project Overview

A comprehensive monthly KPI scoring system designed to evaluate individual staff performance across a team of 20+ nail technicians. Built and managed by the location manager to ensure transparent, data-driven performance reviews and bonus calculations.

> *“A transparent, clear, and fair evaluation of your work — every month.”*

-----

## What This System Tracks

Performance is calculated from 5 weighted indicators plus adjustments:

|Indicator              |How It’s Calculated                             |Weight|
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------|------|
|**Profitability**      |(Revenue − Materials − Salary) / Revenue × 100% |15%   |
|**Client Retention**   |Repeat clients / Total clients × 100%           |35%   |
|**Workload**           |Hours worked / Total salon hours × 100%         |10%   |
|**Workday Utilisation**|Days worked / Total working days in month       |10%   |
|**Weekend Utilisation**|Weekend days worked / Total weekend days × 100% |10%   |
|**PFT Achievement**    |Revenue / (Hours × £40 or £42 for senior) × 100%|20%   |

-----

## Scoring Logic

Each metric is scored on a points scale, multiplied by its weight → summed into a Total Performance Score (0–80).

### Location Financial Target Adjustment

Applied on top of the weighted score:

|Location Target %|Score Adjustment|
|-----------------|----------------|
|100%+            |+1 point        |
|95% – 99.99%     |−5 points       |
|80% – 94.99%     |−10 points      |
|Below 80%        |−25 points      |

### Google Reviews Bonus

+1 point per verified positive Google review received during the month.

### Complaint Penalties

|Complaint Category                       |Deduction             |
|-----------------------------------------|----------------------|
|Critical (injury, major quality failure) |−3 points each        |
|Medium (material detachment, poor finish)|−2 points each        |
|Low (minor quality issue)                |−1 point each         |
|Cost of repair                           |−1 point per £10 spent|

-----

## Performance Categories & Bonuses

|Category       |Score Range|Outcome         |
|---------------|-----------|----------------|
|⭐ Top Performer|65 – 80    |KPI bonus paid  |
|✅ Reliable     |50 – 64    |Standard salary |
|🔄 Needs Focus  |25 – 49    |1-to-1 review   |
|❗ At Risk      |0 – 24     |Performance plan|

Bonus payment date: 20th of the month (alongside first-half salary).  
Minimum eligibility: 10 working days in the reporting month.

- Revenue: £3,537 | Retention: 37.78% (low) | Location FT: below 80% → −25pts
- Result: 26.9 — Needs Focus, no bonus
